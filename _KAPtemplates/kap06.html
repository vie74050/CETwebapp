KAP 6: Professionalism

Practices within the parameters of BCCNM, Professional Standards and Code of Ethics as licensed nurses Practices within the parameters of specialtyspecific practice standards Utilizes unit-specific clinical policies and procedures and decision support tools in providing nursing care Accurately assesses own competence prior to performing specialty- specific nursing actions Accurately assesses own strengths and limitations, and develops strategies to address self-identified learning needs Seeks appropriate assistance when necessary Maintains confidentiality (patient, family, and colleagues) Advocates for patients and families in the provision of nursing care
