KAP 7: Communication

Communicates respectfully and effectively with patients and family members Tailors communication to specific patient needs or communication barriers (e.g., mechanical ventilation, language barriers, developmental stage) Keeps patients and families appropriately apprised of patient health status and plan of care Shares relevant information in a concise, systematic, and timely manner with healthcare team members Articulates and documents relevant findings in a clear, comprehensive, systematic, and time-sensitive manner Evaluates effectiveness of own communication with patients, family, and health care team members
