KAP 3: Integration of Theory to Practice

Works in partnership with the patient and family members to provide safe, competent, and ethical nursing care. Integrates specialty-specific theory, knowledge and frameworks into assessment and clinical decision-making. Recognizes family and patient needs and utilizes equity-oriented frameworks to provide equity-oriented care to patients and families (e.g., culturally safe, traumainformed and respectfully inclusive, family-oriented care) Considers the impact of the specialty-specific environment on the experience of patients and families. Promotes the patient's comfort and optimizes healing using relevant specialtyspecific knowledge.
